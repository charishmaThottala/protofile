INDIAN STYLE PANEER BRIYANI

BY charishma

Ingredients

  • basmati rice - 1cup
  • paneer - 200g
  • curd - 1/2 cup
  • onion - 1(larged)

steps:

  1. cook rice
  2. marinate paneer
  3. prepare masala
    • heat oil in a pan
    • fry onions
    • add green chillies
    • add mint,corinder leaves
  4. layer briyani
